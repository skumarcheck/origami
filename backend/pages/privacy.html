Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Foldiverse is a kid-friendly origami learning app. We take your privacy seriously. We collect as little as possible and never sell your data.

1. What We Collect

Account info:

  • Name — to say hello to you in the app
  • Email — so you can log in
  • Password — stored encrypted (we can never see it)
  • Skill level — so we show the right origami for you

How you use the app:

  • Which origami you finish, your favourites, and your XP points
  • This is only so you can pick up where you left off

Payments:

Payments go through Google Play (Android) or PayPal (web). We never see your card number or bank details.

2. How We Use It

  • Let you log in and use the app
  • Show origami that match your skill level
  • Track your progress and XP
  • Manage your subscription

We do NOT show ads. We do NOT sell your data. Ever.

3. Kids' Privacy

Foldiverse is for all ages, including children. We follow:

  • COPPA (USA children's privacy law)
  • UK Children's Code
  • GDPR (EU privacy rules for kids)

For children under 13, we recommend a parent creates the account. We don't collect extra info, show ads, or share kids' data with anyone.

4. Keeping Your Data Safe

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are encrypted. All connections use HTTPS. We only keep your data while you have an account.

5. Who We Share With

  • Google Play / PayPal — only for payments
  • Law enforcement — only if the law says we must

Nobody else. We don't sell, rent, or trade your info.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • See what data we have about you
  • Fix anything that's wrong
  • Delete your account and all your data
  • Cancel your subscription any time

7. Cookies & Tracking

We don't use cookies, tracking pixels, or follow you around the internet. None of that.

8. Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top and let you know in the app.

9. Contact Us

Email: privacy@foldiverse.com

We aim to reply within 48 hours.
