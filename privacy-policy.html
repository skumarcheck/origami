Foldiverse Privacy Policy

Start with paper. End with magic.

Last updated: April 2026

Foldiverse is a kid-friendly origami learning app. We take your privacy and your children's privacy very seriously. We collect as little information as possible and never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name - to personalise your experience
  • Email address - for account login and important updates
  • Password - stored securely (encrypted, we can never see it)
  • Skill level and age range - to show you the right origami projects

Usage Information

We track which origami projects you complete, your favourites, and your progress. This is only to help you pick up where you left off and show your achievements.

Payment Information

If you subscribe to Premium, payments are processed by Google Play (Android) or PayPal (web). We never see or store your credit card number, bank details, or payment card information. All payment processing is handled securely by these third-party services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Let you log in and use the app
  • Show origami projects that match your skill level
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Manage your subscription
  • Send important account updates (very rarely)

We do NOT use your information for advertising. We do NOT sell your data to anyone. Ever.

3. Children's Privacy

Foldiverse is designed for users of all ages, including children. We comply with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act - USA)
  • UK Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code)
  • GDPR-K (General Data Protection Regulation for children - EU)

For children under 13:

  • We recommend a parent or guardian creates the account
  • We do not collect more information than needed
  • We do not show targeted advertising
  • We do not share children's data with third parties
  • Parents can request to see, change, or delete their child's data at any time

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on protected servers. Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard methods (bcrypt). We use HTTPS for all data transfers. We keep your data only as long as you have an account with us.

5. Data Sharing

We only share data with:

  • Google Play / PayPal - to process payments (they have their own privacy policies)
  • Law enforcement - only if legally required

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone else.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • See your data - request a copy of what we store about you
  • Fix your data - correct any wrong information
  • Delete your data - request we delete your account and all data
  • Export your data - get your data in a readable format
  • Cancel your subscription - at any time through the app or payment provider

7. Cookies and Tracking

The Foldiverse app does not use cookies or tracking pixels. We do not track your activity outside of the app. We do not use analytics tools that follow you around the internet.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top and notify you in the app. We will never make changes that reduce your privacy protections without giving you clear notice.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, your data, or want to make a request about your child's data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@foldiverse.com

App: Foldiverse

We aim to respond within 48 hours.
